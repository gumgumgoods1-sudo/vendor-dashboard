editable fieldauto-calculated
Tip: a trade can involve several cards on either side (e.g. a customer trading 3 lower-value cards for 1 high-end card) — use the "+ add…" picker in the Traded Out / Received cells to add every card involved, and use Cash In / Cash Out to record any cash that changed hands to balance the trade.
Trade Profit = the cash this trade nets (Cash In − Cash Out) — the amount it adds to Total Actual Profit. A pure card-for-card swap shows $0 here because profit on the cards you took in is realized later, when you sell them (it flows through Sales profit, and the upside shows in Potential Profit). A trade linked to a sale shows $0 too — its profit is counted on that sale instead.
